Stuff to Do in France

Top Locations to Visit: The French Riviera, the Eiffel Tower, and the Louvre Museum

France has lots to offer! We recommend visiting the world-famous Louvre Museum, where you can see incredible artwork including the famous Mona Lisa.

You should also visit the iconic Eiffel Tower, relax along the beautiful French Riviera, and enjoy a scenic cruise down the Seine River while exploring the city of Paris.
