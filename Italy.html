Stuff to Do in Italy

Top Locations to Visit: The Colosseum in Rome, the Amalfi Coast, Pompeii, the Leaning Tower of Pisa, and the Dolomites

Italy has so much to offer! We recommend visiting La Pergola, a Michelin-starred restaurant in Rome, where you can enjoy some of Italy's finest cuisine.

Explore the historic Colosseum in Rome, visit the famous Leaning Tower of Pisa, and enjoy the breathtaking scenery of the Amalfi Coast and the ancient city of Pompeii.

If you love the outdoors, don't miss the beautiful Dolomite Mountains, where you can enjoy hiking, skiing, and even hang gliding.
