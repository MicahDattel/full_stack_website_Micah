Stuff to do in Italy

Top Locations to travel: The Colosseum In Rome, Amalfi Coast & Pompei In Naples, Italy, and Leaning Tower Of Pisa, in Pisa, Italy

Italy has lots to do! Here are our recommendations: Try out La Pergola, a Michelin Star Restaurant in Rome. Trust me, the food will not disappoint.

Check out one of the wonders of the world, the historic Colosseum in Rome. And see the beautiful Leaning Tower of Pisa in Pisa, and the Amalfi Coast in Naples, Italy.

And lastly, check out the Dolomites with the beautiful mountains and lots of activities to do like hang gliding.
