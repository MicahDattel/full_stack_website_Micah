Stuff to do in NYC

Top Locations to travel: Times Square, Wall Street, and Brooklyn Bridge

There is much to do in NYC. We recommend you take trips to Rockefeller Center in Manhattan at 45 Rockefeller Plaza, New York, and Times Square at One Times Square, New York, NY 10036.

For shopping, the Hilton Hotel in Times Square is great for your stay. Take a trip to the historic Statue Of Liberty; along the way you'll shop in some of the franchise's largest and best high-quality stores, and explore the magic of NYC.
