Stuff to Do in Japan

Top Locations to Visit: Super Nintendo World (Osaka), Mount Fuji, Kyoto Temples, Kerama National Park, and the Bullet Train

Japan offers an amazing mix of modern attractions and historic culture. One of the top places to visit is Super Nintendo World at Universal Studios in Osaka.

You can also enjoy the breathtaking view of Mount Fuji, explore ancient temples in Kyoto, and experience one of the fastest trains in the world on the famous Bullet Train.

For nature lovers, Kerama National Park in Okinawa offers crystal-clear waters and the chance to see marine life like sea turtles.
