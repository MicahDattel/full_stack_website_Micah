Stuff to do in Japan

Top Locations to travel: Super Nintendo World in Osaka, Mount Fuji in Honshu Island, Try Mochi from Nakatanidou, Kyoto's Historic Temple, Try out the Bullet Train

There is much to do in Japan! Try out the Super Nintendo World at Universal Studios in Osaka, then try watching the beautiful scenic view of Mount Fuji on Honshu Island.

Go swimming with turtles in Kerama National Park in Okinawa, try fresh Mochi from Nakatanidou, and explore Kyoto's historic temples.
